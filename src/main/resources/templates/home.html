Who are we

Due to the global pandemic, all the recent conferences have to be conducted online. To schedule the online presentation sessions, the organiser needs to consider the time zone, the topic and the country differences. Ideally, the participants from the same (or closer) time zones and the same (or the similar) topics should be grouped into the same sessions.
